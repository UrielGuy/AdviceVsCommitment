A Cautionary Tale

Behold, the danger of commitment

And lo, in the scorching summer sun, a tempestuous figure named Elisa emerged, casting her shadow upon the hallowed grounds of Burning Man. Filled with fervor and conviction, she proclaimed her unwavering commitment to aid the seekers of creative ventures that adorned the playa.

Yet, little did Elisa know that her proclamation would unleash a cataclysm of chaos upon the very fabric of Burning Man. As she fervently devoted herself to each project, her commitment became an inescapable snare, ensnaring the dreams of others in a web of despair.

The once harmonious community, seeking guidance and support, found themselves entangled in Elisa's promises. Their hopes, infused with anticipation, were swiftly dashed as her commitment manifested as an oppressive burden. Each project, suffocated by her unyielding presence, withered like parched foliage in the searing desert winds.

Witness the wrath of the desert gods, as they cast their vengeful gaze upon Elisa's ill-fated commitment. From the depths of the abyss arose the Four Horsemen of Armageddon, mounted upon their spectral steeds. Pestilence, Famine, War, and Death rode forth, heralding the arrival of inevitable destruction.

Pestilence unleashed its toxic influence, infecting the spirits of collaboration and harmony that once thrived within the community. Famine cast its malevolent shadow, starving the projects of the vital freedom they required to flourish. War ignited, as alliances fractured and discord reigned supreme, borne from the oppressive weight of Elisa's unwavering commitment. And Death, the harbinger of finality, claimed the shattered dreams and aspirations, leaving only barren remnants in its wake.

In the heart of the turmoil, Elisa beheld the magnitude of her grievous error. She bore witness to the anguish and devastation wrought by her zealous commitment, forever condemned by the weight of her unyielding promises. Alas, her summer transformed into a desolate wasteland, bereft of joy and salvation. She stood amidst the ruins, a solitary figure haunted by the consequences of her misguided devotion.
