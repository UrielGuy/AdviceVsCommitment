Dr Seuss

In the town of Hoboken,

Lived a girl named Elisa, so open.

She was a helper, that much was true,

But summer was coming, and she had plans too.

One sunny day, as she walked by the sea,

A group of people came running to her, so full of glee.

"Elisa, Elisa, please help us, we're in need!

We need your commitment, your time, indeed!"

Elisa thought for a moment, then said with a smile,

"I'll give you advice, but I won't walk the mile.

I have plans for summer, I hope you can see,

I want to enjoy it, just like you and me."

The people were surprised, but they understood,

That Elisa needed time for herself, as she should.

"Thank you, Elisa, for being so kind,

Your advice will help us, we won't be far behind!"

Elisa felt happy, she could enjoy summer,

And the people were grateful, they didn't feel like a bummer.

So let this be a lesson to all,

To be helpful, but also answer your own call.

Give advice, and enjoy the sun,
